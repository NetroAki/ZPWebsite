Who am I?

Hi my name is Zachary Palmer.
I am a music production and composition teacher from Lancashire.
I have been working within music production for the last
9 years and I worked full time as a music producer
for 2 years until I started to focus on teaching.

What is my experience?

I have worked and trained many young professionals
from sound design and mixing to Music and Song structure.
I work in all styles but I have a specialty with electronic music
I have worked as a youth worker for 7 years and have experience
teaching not only music production to young people
but a variety of instruments and how to use them within a live environment.

How does it work?

I work with all ages and do remote lessons, group lessons and in-person lessons.
So I can come to a location of your choice and teach there (all ages)
or you can come and learn at my home studio (11 years & upwards).
In both instances we can use my equipment or your own.
There is no extra charge on using my equipment and is part of the price.

How do you teach?

my teaching style is all about showing the fundamental
ideas to the student and then using that idea practically
to create an idea/composition to help them
fully understand the use of that idea.

What are your session lengths?

I do half an hour and hour long lessons for individuals.
with a recommendation for young people under
the age of 11 doing half an hour lessons.
I also do half an hour, hour, half day,
and full day lessons for groups
